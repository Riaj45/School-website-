About,
SAGARBARH BANDHAB SATYESWAR VIDYABHAWAN:

SAGARBARH BANDHAB SATYESWAR VIDYABHAWAN was established in 1946 and it is managed by the Department of Education.
It is located in Rural area. It is located in PANSKURA -II block of PURBA MEDINIPUR district of West Bengal.
The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12.
The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section.
The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Bengali is the medium of instructions in this school.
This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
